Coaching

Enlifted Coach

We all carry stories — about who we are, what we deserve, and why we're stuck. Enlifted coaching is a narrative-based method that uses writing and breathwork to surface those stories and work through them. It's not therapy — it's a structured, practical process for people ready to move.

I work with professionals navigating transitions, burnout, or the quiet sense that something isn't aligned.

  • ✍️Writing exercises to surface the stories running the show
  • 🌬️Breathwork to process what writing surfaces
  • 🔁60-minute sessions, available virtually
